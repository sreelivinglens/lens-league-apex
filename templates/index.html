The Honest Part

The score keeps the feedback honest.

Every photo gets a score. Not to rank you against other photographers — but to give the feedback a spine.

Without a score, feedback is just opinion. With a score, you can see whether you are actually improving across ten uploads, a hundred, a year of work.

HOW THE SCORING WORKS →
Emotional Impact

Most systems evaluate sharpness, composition, and light. Ours also evaluates whether a photograph moves people.

A technically imperfect photograph that stops you breathing scores higher than a technically clean one that says nothing.

That is by design.
