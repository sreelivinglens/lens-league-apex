How it works
One improvement. Every time.

Most feedback overwhelms you with everything at once. We give you one thing — the most important one — and let you shoot with it.

01
Upload your photograph

Any genre. Any skill level. Camera or phone. You get a score across five dimensions — including emotional impact — and one specific improvement note written the way a senior photographer would say it.

02
Apply it. Shoot again.

Upload your next photograph. Watch your score move. Over ten uploads, a hundred, a year of work — the pattern becomes visible. That pattern is your growth as a photographer.

{# ━━ BEAT 3 — SCORE SECTION ━━ #}
The honest part
The score keeps the feedback honest.

Every photo gets a score. Not to rank you against other photographers — but to give the feedback a spine. Without a score, feedback is just opinion. With a score, you can see whether you are actually improving across ten uploads, a hundred, a year of work.

Emotional impact

Most systems evaluate sharpness, composition, and light. Ours also evaluates whether a photograph moves people. A technically imperfect photograph that stops you breathing scores higher than a technically clean one that says nothing. That is by design.
