Common questions
How do I cancel my subscription?
Log in, go to your Dashboard, and click "Cancel subscription" in the subscription panel. No email needed. Access continues until the end of your billing period. See our Refund Policy for details.
I was charged but my subscription is not active.
Use the form above — select "Billing or refund request" and include your registered email and payment reference. We will resolve it within 2 working days.
My image was flagged as AI-generated but it is not.
Use the form above — select "Image integrity appeal" and include your username and image ID. Attach your original RAW file or camera roll metadata if you have it. We review all appeals.
How do I delete my account?
Use the form above — select "Account deletion request". We will confirm deletion within 5 working days. Note that submitted competition images may be retained for integrity record-keeping per our Terms & Conditions.
