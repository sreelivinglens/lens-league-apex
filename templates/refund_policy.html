Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Lens League Apex

Summary: You can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. No questions asked. Access continues until the end of your current billing period. Competition entry fees are non-refundable once the submission window opens. For any billing issue, email sreeks@gmail.com.

01 Monthly & Annual Subscriptions

All subscription plans — Camera League, Mobile League, and any Mentor on Demand tiers — are billed on a recurring basis.

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard without contacting support. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You retain full access to your subscribed features until that date.

No partial refunds are issued for unused days within a billing period.

Annual plans

If you purchase an annual subscription, you may request a full refund within 7 days of payment provided you have not scored more than 4 images under the plan. After 7 days or after 4 images have been scored, no refund is available for annual plans.

02 Competition Entry Fees

Entry fees paid for the Photographer of the Year (POTY), Body of Work (BOW), and Open Competition are non-refundable once the submission window for that competition cycle has opened.

If a competition is cancelled by Lens League Apex before the submission window opens, a full refund will be issued within 7 working days to your original payment method.

03 Image Top-up Credits

Image top-up credits are non-refundable once purchased. Credits are valid for 90 days from the date of purchase and will expire after that period.

04 Mentor on Demand

If you have subscribed to a Mentor on Demand tier and your assigned juror has not yet reviewed any of your images in the current billing month, you may request a cancellation and full refund for that month by writing to us within 5 days of your billing date.

Once any image review has been delivered, that month's Mentor fee is non-refundable.

05 Duplicate or Erroneous Charges

If you believe you have been charged in error or charged twice for the same plan, write to us within 14 days of the charge. We will investigate and issue a refund within 7 working days if the error is confirmed.

06 Platform-Initiated Cancellations

If your account is suspended or banned under our three-strike integrity policy, no refund is issued for the remaining subscription period. This is stated in our Terms & Conditions which you agreed to at registration.

07 How to Request a Refund

Email us at sreeks@gmail.com

  • Subject line: Refund Request — [your registered email] — [plan name]
  • We respond within 2 working days
  • Approved refunds are processed within 7 working days to your original payment method
