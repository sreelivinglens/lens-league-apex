  • No cash value. Points cannot be exchanged for cash, transferred between accounts, or sold.
  • Annual reset. 10% of your balance resets on 31 December — but only if you haven't uploaded in the last 6 months. Stay active and your full balance carries forward.

  • Subject to availability. Programme credits (Open, BOW, Annual Excellence Award) are only redeemable during the relevant window. Mentor sessions are subject to mentor availability.
  • Rate changes. {{ platform_name }} may adjust redemption rates or add new redemption options at any time with a minimum of 14 days' notice to active subscribers.
  • Payment infrastructure. Points redemption for cash-equivalent items is subject to payment infrastructure being operational. {{ platform_name }}'s obligation is to activate redemption within 30 days of payment infrastructure going live.
