Shipping & Returns Policy

Shutter League is a fully digital platform. This policy explains how digital service delivery works and what happens if something goes wrong.

Last updated: 29 April 2026  ·  Effective immediately
Shutter League sells digital services only. We do not sell, manufacture, or ship any physical goods. There is nothing to ship and therefore no shipping charges, delivery timelines, or courier-related concerns applicable to any transaction on this platform.

Digital Service Delivery

All products and services sold on Shutter League are delivered digitally and immediately upon successful payment. These include:

  • Subscription Plans (Camera Track / Mobile Track) — Access is activated instantly upon payment confirmation. Members can begin uploading and accessing platform features immediately.
  • Open Programme Submission Fees — Submission is confirmed instantly upon payment. The submitted image enters the programme immediately.
  • Body of Work Entry Fees — Submission access is granted immediately upon payment for non-subscribers.
  • Top-up Credits — Additional upload or challenge slots are credited to the member's account immediately.

Since all services are digital and delivered instantly, there is no shipping, tracking, or physical delivery involved at any stage.

No Physical Returns

As Shutter League does not deal in physical goods, there is no returns process applicable. Members cannot return a subscription, programme submission, or any other digital service once it has been activated or used.

If a member believes they were charged incorrectly or did not receive the service they paid for, they should contact us at info@shutterleague.com and we will investigate and resolve the issue promptly.

Failed or Incomplete Transactions

If a payment is deducted from your account but the corresponding service (subscription activation, programme submission, or credit) is not reflected on your Shutter League account within 24 hours, please contact us immediately:

  • Email: info@shutterleague.com
  • Include your registered email address and the transaction reference number

We will verify the transaction and either activate the service or initiate a full refund within 5–7 business days.

Refund Policy

For full details on refunds, cancellations, and eligible scenarios, please refer to our Refund Policy.

Grievance Redressal

For any unresolved complaints or disputes related to payments, service delivery, or this policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: info@shutterleague.com
  • Response time: Within 2 business days
  • Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India
