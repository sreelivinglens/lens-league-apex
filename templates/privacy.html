Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Lens League Apex

Summary: We collect only what we need to run the platform. We never sell your data. Your images remain yours. Contact sreeks@gmail.com for any privacy questions.

01 Who We Are

Lens League Apex is a photography scoring and competition platform operated from India. Contact: sreeks@gmail.com

02 What We Collect

  • Account information — name, email address, username, password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
  • Profile information — country, state, city, declared camera or phone brand
  • Images you upload — the photograph, title, genre, location, and subject notes you provide
  • EXIF metadata — extracted automatically from uploaded images (camera make/model, date taken, settings). Used for league integrity verification only. Never shown publicly.
  • Payment information — processed entirely by Razorpay. We store only the subscription or order ID. We never see or store your card details.
  • Usage data — pages visited, images scored, challenge submissions. Used to improve the platform.

03 How We Use Your Data

  • To score your images using the APEX DDI Engine
  • To display your public portfolio and leaderboard standing
  • To process subscription payments and competition entries
  • To send you challenge notifications and results emails
  • To verify camera/phone league integrity via EXIF checks
  • To improve the scoring engine and platform features

We never sell your data to advertisers or third parties. Lens League Apex is ad-free. Brand sponsorships are media buys attached to challenge weeks — sponsors do not receive any user data.

04 Your Images

You retain full copyright over all images you upload. By uploading, you grant Lens League Apex a limited licence to display your images on the platform, in leaderboards, on results pages, and in promotional materials that reference the platform (e.g. social media posts showcasing winning entries).

We do not sell, license, or transfer your images to third parties. Sponsors do not receive access to your images.

You can delete your images at any time from your dashboard. Note that images submitted to competitions may be retained in competition records for integrity purposes as stated in our Terms & Conditions.

05 EXIF Data

EXIF metadata is extracted from your uploaded images to verify that you are competing in the correct league (Camera or Mobile). This data is used only for integrity checks and is never displayed publicly or shared with any third party.

If your EXIF data contradicts your declared league, you may receive a warning or, after three mismatches, a temporary suspension of contest access pending admin review.

06 Cookies and Storage

We use session cookies to keep you logged in. These are secure, HTTP-only cookies set on our domain only. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics beyond what is necessary to operate the platform.

07 Third-Party Services

  • Razorpay — payment processing. Subject to Razorpay's privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare R2 — image storage. Images are stored on Cloudflare's infrastructure.
  • Brevo — transactional email delivery. Your email address is used only to send platform notifications.
  • Google OAuth — optional login via Google. We receive only your name, email, and Google ID.
  • Anthropic Claude — AI scoring engine. Images are sent to Anthropic's API for scoring. No personally identifiable information is included in API requests.
  • Railway — hosting infrastructure. Application logs may be retained for up to 7 days.

08 Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 5 working days. Images submitted to competitions may be retained in anonymised competition records.

09 Your Rights

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for email communications (reply to any email with "unsubscribe")

To exercise any of these rights, email sreeks@gmail.com with subject line Privacy Request — [your username].

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the platform evolves. Significant changes will be communicated by email. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about your privacy?  Contact us  or email sreeks@gmail.com
