01 Who We Are

Lens League Apex is a photography scoring and competition platform operated from Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. We operate the website at lens-league-apex-production.up.railway.app and any associated domains. When this policy says "we", "us", or "Lens League Apex", it refers to the operator of this platform.

For any privacy-related questions, corrections, or deletion requests, contact us at verify@lensleague.com. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

03 How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for the purposes described above. More specifically:

  • Running the platform — scoring your images, displaying leaderboards, managing subscriptions, processing contest entries.
  • Communicating with you — prize notifications, RAW verification requests, account security alerts, and responses to your support enquiries. We do not send unsolicited marketing emails.
  • Integrity checks — EXIF analysis, perceptual hash duplicate detection, AI-generation detection, and League mismatch monitoring to keep the platform fair.
  • Improving the engine — admin-reviewed calibration notes and REF example images are used to calibrate the Apex DDI Engine. No personally identifiable data is used for AI model training.
  • Legal compliance — retaining records for tax purposes (prize disbursements) and responding to valid legal requests.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any automated decision-making that has legal or similarly significant effects on you.

05 Your Images

You retain full copyright in every image you upload. By uploading, you grant Lens League Apex a non-exclusive licence to display, score, and use winning or shortlisted images for platform promotion — always with attribution. See the Terms & Conditions — Section 09 for the full IP clause.

Where your images are stored

Uploaded images are processed (resized to 960px on the longer side for AI scoring) and stored on Cloudflare R2. The processing thumbnail is what the scoring engine sees; your original file is not retained on our servers — we resize at upload time. We strongly recommend you retain your original files for RAW verification purposes.

Who can see your images

  • Public images — appear on the leaderboard, your public profile, and share pages. You control this setting per image at upload time.
  • Hidden images — scored privately, visible only to you. Not shown anywhere publicly.
  • Peer rating pool images — shown to other subscribers for blind rating. Only the image is shown; your name and score are hidden during the rating process.
  • Flagged or under-review images — hidden from all public views until admin review is complete.

AI processing

Your image is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for DDI scoring. The image is transmitted securely over HTTPS. Anthropic processes the image to generate the score and analysis, and does not use submitted images to train its models under its standard API terms. We do not store the AI's intermediate processing data — only the final score, module breakdown, and analysis text.

06 Data Security

We take reasonable and appropriate measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS everywhere — all traffic between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS.
  • Secure cookies — session cookies are set with Secure, HttpOnly, and SameSite=Lax flags.
  • Password hashing — admin passwords are stored as bcrypt hashes. We never store plaintext passwords.
  • Database access control — the database is not publicly accessible; only the application server can connect.
  • No card data stored — all payment processing is delegated entirely to Razorpay. We store only the Razorpay subscription ID.
No system is perfectly secure. In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users by email as promptly as practicable and take immediate steps to contain the breach. We will also notify the relevant authority as required under applicable Indian data protection law.

07 How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Active accounts — data is retained for as long as your account exists.
  • Deleted accounts — all personally identifiable data is permanently deleted within 30 days of a deletion request. Anonymised aggregate statistics (e.g. genre average scores) that contain no personal identifiers may be retained indefinitely.
  • Prize records — winner details and payment records are retained for 7 years as required for tax compliance under Indian law.
  • Server logs — IP address logs are retained for up to 90 days for security purposes, then automatically deleted.

09 Cookies & Tracking

We use only one cookie: a session cookie that keeps you logged in. It contains no personal data — only a secure session identifier. It is set as HttpOnly (not accessible to JavaScript), Secure (HTTPS only), and expires when you log out or after an inactivity period.

We do not use:

  • Analytics cookies (no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar)
  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Third-party cookies of any kind

Because we use only a functional session cookie, we do not display a cookie consent banner. If this changes, we will update this policy and implement appropriate consent mechanisms.

10 Children's Privacy

If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us immediately at verify@lensleague.com and we will delete the account promptly.
