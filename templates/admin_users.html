UAT Access Flow

  1. Ask testers to register normally at the live URL — they go through the standard sign-up flow.
  2. Find them in the table below. Select their track (Camera or Mobile — whichever they're testing).
  3. Click Activate UAT — they instantly get 10 scored images + full contest access.
  4. They test the full photographer journey: upload → score → rating cards → leaderboard → contest entry.
  5. When UAT is complete, click Revoke — they revert to the standard free tier (3 lifetime images, no contest access).
