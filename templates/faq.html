📐   Scoring & the DDI Engine
The Dimensional Diagnostic Index is a science-based score across five independent modules: DoD Depth of Difficulty, DIS Visual Disruption, DM Decisive Moment, WON Wonder Factor, and AQ Affective Quotient. Each image is scored 0–9.9. Module weights are calibrated per genre, so a Wildlife image is measured differently from a Wedding or Macro image. The final score is a weighted sum with safeguards applied.
Five tiers map to fixed score bands: Apprentice 0–5.0  ·  Practitioner 5.1–7.5  ·  Master 7.6–8.9  ·  Grandmaster 9.0–9.6  ·  Legend 9.7–9.9. Tier boundaries are fixed — they don't shift with the platform population. A Master-tier image today means the same thing it will mean in five years.
No. Your numerical DDI score is private — only you can see it on your dashboard and image detail pages. The public leaderboard and share pages display tier classification only (e.g. "Master" or "Grandmaster"), never the raw number. This is a core platform policy and will not change.
AQ measures the emotional truth and human resonance of an image — the feeling it creates in the viewer, not its technical attributes. It is the soul of the score. Images with AQ ≥ 8.0 receive the ★ Soul Bonus, which removes all technical penalties from the final calculation. A technically imperfect image that genuinely moves people will always outscore a technically perfect but emotionally empty one.

🔒   Privacy & Your Data
We collect only what's needed to run the platform: your email address (via Google OAuth), display name, country / state / city (for leaderboard ranking), and EXIF metadata embedded in your uploaded images (camera model, date taken, exposure settings). We do not collect your GPS location, phone number, government ID, or financial card details. Payment processing is handled entirely by Razorpay — we never see or store your card data. Full details are in the Privacy Policy.
