Frequently Asked Questions

{{ platform_name }} — Everything you need to know

01 Getting Started

What is Lens League Apex?
Lens League Apex is a photography scoring and competition platform. Every image you upload is scored by the Apex DDI Engine — a scientific, AI-powered system that evaluates your photography across 5 dimensions. No human judges, no bias, consistent feedback every time.
What is the DDI score?
DDI stands for Dimensional Diagnostic Index. It's a composite score from 0–10 based on five dimensions: Depth of Detail, Wonder, Disruption, Depth of Mood, and Aesthetic Quality. Each dimension is weighted differently per genre.
How many free images do I get?
3 free scored images — no credit card required. After that, subscribe to the Camera or Mobile track to continue uploading and competing.
Do I need to use a camera? Can I use my phone?
Both are welcome. Camera and Mobile photographers compete in separate leagues with their own leaderboards, POTY tracks, and prize pools — same DDI engine, no gear bias.

02 Scoring

How long does scoring take?
Most images are scored within 30–60 seconds of upload. During peak periods it may take a few minutes. You'll see your score appear on your dashboard automatically.
Can I rescore an image?
No. Each image is scored once. This protects the integrity of the leaderboard and competition results. If you upload a better version of an image, it is treated as a new upload and uses one of your monthly scored image credits.
Why did my image score lower than I expected?
The DDI Engine scores against genre-specific criteria. The score breakdown on your image detail page shows exactly which dimensions scored lower and includes an Apex Byline — a specific improvement recommendation. Use that as your guide.
What are the score tiers?
Rookie — 0.0 to 3.9  |  Shooter — 4.0 to 4.9  |  Contender — 5.0 to 5.9  |  Craftsman — 6.0 to 6.9  |  Maverick — 7.0 to 7.9  |  Master — 8.0 to 8.9  |  Grandmaster — 9.0 to 9.6  |  Legend — 9.7 and above

03 Subscriptions & Billing

What plans are available?
Free — 3 lifetime scored images, full DDI analysis, shareable rating card.
Camera Track — ₹599/month or ₹4,999/year. 4 scored images per month, full contest access, peer rating, POTY eligibility.
Mobile Track — ₹299/month or ₹2,499/year. Same features as Camera, dedicated Mobile league.
Do unused monthly images roll over?
No. Your 4 monthly images reset at the start of each calendar month. Unused images do not carry forward.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to your Dashboard and select Manage Subscription, or visit the cancellation page directly. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fee.
Can I switch from Mobile to Camera track?
Yes — cancel your current subscription and subscribe to the other track. Your scored images, scores, and history are retained.
What is the refund policy?
See our full Refund & Cancellation Policy. In brief: refunds are considered for technical failures, duplicate charges, or unauthorised charges. Requests must be submitted within 14 days.

04 Competitions & POTY

How do monthly contests work?
Every scored image automatically enters the monthly contest for its genre and track. No separate submission needed. Winners are determined by DDI score at the end of each month.
What is POTY?
Photographer of the Year. Your POTY score is the average of your top-6 DDI scores per genre, tracked across the full platform year. You must be active across at least 6 months to qualify. Camera and Mobile compete separately. Total prize pool: ₹50,00,000.
What is the Open Competition?
An annual competition where Camera and Mobile photographers compete together in the same pool. One winner per genre, plus a Grand Open Winner selected by jury. Entry fee: ₹50 per image per genre. Requires an active subscription.
What are Weekly Challenges?
A new photography prompt is released every Monday. Submit your best image by Sunday 23:59 UTC. Free to enter. Challenge winners qualify for POTY tracking.

05 Images & Copyright

Who owns my images?
You do. Uploading to Lens League Apex does not transfer copyright. We store your images to provide the scoring and portfolio service. See our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions for full details.
Can I download my images back?
Your original uploaded images are stored on our platform. Contact us at {{ contact_email }} if you need access to your original files.
What happens if my image is flagged as AI-generated?
The platform checks for AI-generated images as part of the upload process. If your image is flagged, it will not appear on the public leaderboard and your score will be held. You can appeal via the platform — all appeals are reviewed by the admin team.
What is RAW verification?
For high-scoring images and competition winners, we may request your original RAW file to verify authenticity. This protects the integrity of the leaderboard. Failure to submit a RAW file within the verification window will result in the score being withheld.

06 Peer Rating

What is peer rating?
Subscribers can rate other photographers' images anonymously. Rating others earns credits, which can be used to enter your own images into the peer rating pool. Peer ratings are shown alongside your DDI score but do not change it.
Can peer ratings affect my DDI score?
No. Your DDI score is set by the Apex DDI Engine and is protected. If peer ratings diverge significantly from your DDI score, a jury review is triggered automatically. Your DDI score is never reduced by peer ratings.
