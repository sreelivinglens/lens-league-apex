The Philosophy

Why a season? Why not just one image?

Every major photography competition in the world asks you to submit your best image. One entry. One moment. One judge's opinion on one day. The best photographer doesn't always win — the luckiest one often does.

"Cricket doesn't crown its best player on the basis of one innings. Chess doesn't decide its champion from a single game. Neither should photography."

A season-long rating system means your standing reflects who you truly are as a photographer — your consistency, your range, your growth. One extraordinary image can lift your average. One off day doesn't destroy it.

The Lens League POTY is the first photography championship built on this principle. Rated by the same engine, the same 5 dimensions, every upload, all year long.

Qualification

How POTY qualification works

POTY qualification is automatic. There's no separate entry, no nomination, no form to fill. Upload, score, and your standing updates in real time.

01
Upload and score throughout the year
Every scored image contributes to your POTY standing. The DDI engine rates each upload across 5 dimensions — Depth of Detail, Wonder, Disruption, Dimensional Mastery, and Aesthetic Quality.
02
Stay active for at least 6 months
You must upload scored images in at least 6 separate calendar months of the platform year. This ensures POTY reflects a genuine sustained commitment, not a one-week burst.
03
Your top-6 scores per genre are averaged
For each genre you shoot in, your best 6 DDI scores are averaged to produce your POTY genre score. Quality over volume — you can't game it by uploading hundreds of mediocre images.
04
Camera and Mobile Leagues are separate
Camera photographers compete in the Camera POTY. Mobile photographers compete in the Mobile POTY. Same engine, same 5 dimensions, same standard — but no gear disadvantage.
05
Top performers by genre qualify for the Grand Prix Gala
The top-ranked photographers in each genre at year-end qualify for the annual POTY Grand Prix — Lens League Apex's flagship event with India's largest photography prize pool.
