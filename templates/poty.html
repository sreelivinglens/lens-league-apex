How Rankings Are Determined

Every image uploaded to Shutter League is evaluated by the Apex DDI Engine — a five-dimension scoring framework covering Degree of Difficulty, Visual Disruption, Decisive Moment, Wonder Factor, and Affective Quotient. Scores are generated automatically and consistently, with no human subjectivity in the initial assessment.

A photographer's annual ranking in each genre is calculated from their best six DDI scores in that genre during the calendar year. The top-20 photographers per genre proceed to jury review, where a panel of photography professionals evaluates the work and confirms the final rankings.

This two-stage process — AI consistency followed by human judgment — is the same standard used by the world's leading photography award programmes.
