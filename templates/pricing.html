Frequently asked questions

FAQ

Can I switch between Camera and Mobile League?

You can upgrade from Mobile to Camera anytime. Your scoring history carries over.

Do unused images roll over to next month?

No. Allocations reset on your billing date.

What if I run out of monthly images?

You can purchase a top-up pack anytime to add more scored uploads.

What's included in the Annual Excellence Award?

Recognition is based on your highest-scoring images across the year. All subscribers are automatically eligible.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Access continues until the end of the billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

UPI, cards, and net banking via PayU. Points can also be used for top-ups and mentor sessions.
