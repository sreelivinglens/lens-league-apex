Common Questions
What counts as a scored image?
Every image you upload that goes through the Apex DDI Engine counts as one scored image. Free users get 6 scored images in their first month and 3 per month thereafter. Subscribers get unlimited scored images.
Do unused images roll over?
Free users get 6 images in their first month and 3 per month thereafter — these reset on the 1st of each month and do not carry forward. Subscribers have no monthly limit.
Can I switch from Mobile to Camera track?
Yes — cancel your current subscription and subscribe to the other track. Your scored images and history remain.
Is there a refund policy?
We offer a full refund within 7 days of subscription if you have not used any scored images in that billing period.
Can I enter the Open Competition on a free plan?
No — Open Competition entry requires an active Camera or Mobile subscription. The image you enter must have been previously scored on your account.
Does the Open Competition have a track split?
No — Camera and Mobile photographers compete in the same pool for each genre. One winner per genre regardless of device.
