Common Questions
What counts as a scored image?
Every image you upload that goes through the Apex DDI Engine counts as one scored image. Rescores of the same image do not count again.
Do unused images roll over?
No — your 4 monthly images reset at the start of each calendar month. Unused images do not carry forward.
Can I switch from Mobile to Camera track?
Yes — cancel your current subscription and subscribe to the other track. Your scored images and history remain.
Is there a refund policy?
