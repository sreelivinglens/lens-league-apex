Frequently Asked Questions
Can I switch between Camera and Mobile League?
You can upgrade from Mobile to Camera anytime. Switching the other direction requires contacting support. Your scoring history carries over.
What if I run out of monthly images?
Purchase a top-up pack (₹99 for Mobile, ₹199 for Camera) — adds 5 scored images to your account for that month. Or wait until next billing cycle.
Do unused images roll over to next month?
No. Your allocation resets on your billing date. Upload regularly to maximize value.
What's included in the Annual Excellence Award?
All subscribers are automatically entered. Your top 6 DDI scores in your strongest genre determine your ranking. See Annual Excellence Award details.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your Dashboard. Access continues until the end of your current billing period. No refunds for partial months.
What payment methods do you accept?
Credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking via PayU. We never store card details — all payments processed through RBI-authorised gateways.
