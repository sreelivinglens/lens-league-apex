Status is days since last upload or deletion. Shape compares each member's first two weeks after joining against their most recent two weeks: "flattened" means they uploaded early and nothing since, "steady" means recent activity holds up, "new" means it's too early to tell, "irregular" means neither pattern clearly fits.
