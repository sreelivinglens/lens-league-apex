Who We Are

Shutter League is a digital platform founded in Bengaluru, India, dedicated to helping photographers improve, compete, and grow. We combine artificial intelligence with human expertise to provide objective, consistent scoring of photographic work across multiple genres.

Our platform is built and operated by a small team of photographers, engineers, and photography educators who believe that every serious photographer deserves honest, detailed feedback on their work — not just likes.

What We Do

Shutter League offers the following services to registered members:

  • AI-Powered Image Scoring (DDI) — Every uploaded photograph is scored across five dimensions: Depth of Detail, Disruption, Dramatic Moment, Wonder, and Aesthetic Quality. Scores are on a 0–100 scale.
  • Weekly Photography Challenges — Themed weekly contests open to all members, with ranked results published every Monday.
  • Photographer of the Year (POTY) — An annual contest tracking each member's top-6 scores per genre across the year.
  • Body of Work (BOW) — An annual submission contest for a curated series of 6–10 images, judged by a human jury.
  • Open Contest — A paid-entry contest open to all members, with no theme restrictions.
  • Brand Contests — Sponsored contests open to subscribed members, free to enter.
  • Peer Rating — Subscribed members can rate each other's work and earn pool access for additional visibility.
  • Subscriptions — Monthly subscription plans (Camera Track and Mobile Track) providing enhanced upload limits, contest access, and platform features.

Our Mission

We exist to give photographers in India — from hobbyists to professionals — a credible, objective benchmark for their work. Photography has long lacked a structured scoring system that photographers can trust and grow with. Shutter League is building that system.

We are committed to transparency in scoring, fairness in contests, and privacy in how we handle member data.

Business Category

Shutter League operates as a digital services platform in the category of Arts, Media & Entertainment — Photography. All transactions on this platform are for digital services including subscription plans, contest entry fees, and platform access. No physical goods are sold or shipped.
