Apex DDI Engine  ·  Dimensional Diagnostic Index

Rated by science.
Not opinion.

The Apex DDI Engine — Dimensional Diagnostic Index — evaluates every photograph across five independently-weighted modules. Each module addresses a distinct dimension of photographic achievement. Together they produce a score that tells you not just how good your image is, but exactly why — and what one change would move it to the next tier.

Like chess ELO Magnus Carlsen has a rating of 2830. The world knows what that means. Your photography now has the same.
Like a report card A single grade tells you nothing. A report card tells you everything. The DDI is the report card your photography has always needed.
The Five Modules
DoD
Depth of Difficulty
Technical and situational difficulty of capturing the image. Environmental constraints, timing precision, subject accessibility. Sharpness IS DoD for Macro. Technique IS DoD for Creative.
DIS
Visual Disruption
How strongly the image breaks from visual convention. Intentional deviation that creates a more powerful or unexpected visual statement — not chaos, but deliberate originality.
DM
Decisive Moment
Timing, irreversibility, and completeness of the captured moment. Whether the shutter was released at the precise instant where all elements align simultaneously.
WON
Wonder Factor
The image's capacity to arrest attention, provoke curiosity, and sustain engagement. Rare behaviour, impossible perspectives, the unseen truth.
AQ
Affective Quotient
The emotional truth and human resonance. A felt experience rather than merely a recorded one. AQ is the soul of the score — it can override technical penalties entirely.

Scoring Safeguards
Soul Bonus
AQ ≥ 8.0 removes all technical penalties. A photograph that moves people cannot be penalised out of recognition.
Humanity Check
AQ < 4.0 adds −1.5 to the AQ component. Technical perfection without emotional truth is not a high score.
Iconic Wall
Scores ≥ 9.0 require both Disruption AND AQ > 8.5. The highest tier cannot be reached by optimising one dimension alone.
Plateau Penalty
DoD ≥ 9.5 + Disruption < 5.0 caps at 7.9. Technical perfection without creative ambition is not mastery.
