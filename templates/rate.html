How Peer Evaluation Works
1
Blind evaluation. You see the image and its interest only — no photographer name, no prior evaluation. Judge purely on what you see.
2
Earn credits. Every submitted evaluation earns 1 credit. Accumulate 5 credits to unlock a peer pool entry for one of your own images.
3
Pool access. When your image is in the peer pool, other subscribers evaluate it. You receive a peer average alongside your evaluation.
4
Divergence triggers jury review. If peer results diverge significantly from the engine evaluation, the image is referred to a jury. Your evaluation is always protected.
5
Bias detection. Systematic patterns (always high, always low, or targeting specific photographers) are flagged automatically and reviewed by admin.
