How Peer Rating Works
1
Blind rating. You see the image and genre only — no photographer name, no DDI score. Rate purely on what you see.
2
Earn credits. Every submitted rating earns 1 credit. Accumulate 5 credits to unlock a peer pool entry for one of your own images.
3
Pool access. When your image is in the peer pool, other subscribers rate it. You receive a peer average score alongside your DDI score.
4
Divergence triggers jury review. If peer scores diverge significantly from the DDI score, the image is referred to a jury. Your DDI score is always protected.
5
Bias detection. Systematic rating patterns (always high, always low, or targeting specific photographers) are flagged automatically and reviewed by admin.
