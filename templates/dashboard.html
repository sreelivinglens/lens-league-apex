Welcome to Shutter League

What is Photographer of the Year (POTY)?

Every photo you upload is scored by our AI engine on a scale of 0‑100. Your top 6 scores in each genre are averaged to build your {{ glink_dark("POTY")|safe }} standing. The photographers with the highest averages at year's end win the title.

📷
Step 1
Upload your best photo to the {{ glink_dark("Weekly Challenge")|safe }}
🌟
Step 2
Receive your {{ glink_dark("DDI")|safe }} score — usually within minutes
🏆
Step 3
Build your top‑6 average and climb the {{ glink_dark("POTY")|safe }} leaderboard

Tiers — as your average climbs you earn ranks: Rookie → Shooter → Contender → Craftsman → Maverick → Master → Grandmaster → Legend. You need at least 6 scored images in a genre before an average is shown, and 24 scored images to qualify for prizes.
