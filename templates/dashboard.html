{# How POTY scoring works — guideline message #}
How your POTY average is calculated
1. Your best 6 scored images in each genre are automatically selected — you don't choose them. The system always picks your highest-scoring 6.
2. A minimum of 6 scored images in a genre is required before an average is shown. With fewer than 6, no average is calculated.
3. Every time you upload a stronger image, it automatically replaces the weakest in your top 6 — raising your average. You can delete images you no longer want from your portfolio.
4. Peer ratings can also improve existing scores — a better blended score on an image already in your top 6 pushes your average up without uploading anything new.
5. You need at least 24 scored images in a genre to qualify for POTY prizes in that genre. The qualifying threshold is announced at Month 9 — you can keep uploading until the year-end deadline.
6. Deleting images does not improve your average. All scored images count toward your POTY record regardless of deletion.
