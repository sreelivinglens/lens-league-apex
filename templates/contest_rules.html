02 Eligibility, Age & Entry

Age Requirement

All competitions are open to participants who are 18 years of age or older at the time of entry. {{ platform_name }} reserves the right to request proof of age at any time. Providing false information regarding age will result in immediate disqualification and forfeiture of any prizes awarded.

General Eligibility

  • Entry is open to all registered {{ platform_name }} subscribers in good standing.
  • Accounts with active policy violations, pending fraud investigations, or suspended status are ineligible.
  • Employees, directors, and immediate family members of {{ platform_name }} are ineligible for prize awards, though they may use the platform.
  • Participants must hold a valid subscription for the duration of the competition cycle in which they are entered.

Monthly Contest & POTY — Automatic Entry

Every image scored by the Apex DDI Engine within the calendar month is automatically entered into the contest for its declared genre and League. No separate entry action is required.

Body of Work — Curated Submission

BOW entry requires deliberate submission of a curated series of 6–12 images. Entry is not automatic. All images must have been previously scored by the Apex DDI Engine under the submitting photographer's account.

Open Competition — Paid Entry

The Open Competition requires deliberate, paid entry. Each entry costs ₹50 per image per genre. A photographer may enter multiple genres but may submit only one image per genre.

03 Image Specifications & Technical Requirements

  • Format: Original JPEG only. Re-saved JPEGs, screenshots, scans of prints, and images exported from social media platforms are not accepted.
  • File size: Maximum 20 MB per file. Files are automatically resized to 960px on the longer side for scoring purposes.
  • Minimum dimensions: 2,000 pixels on the shorter side. Images below this threshold will be rejected.
  • Metadata: Original EXIF metadata must be intact — camera make and model, date and time taken, and exposure settings. Images with stripped or altered EXIF data are flagged as Unverified.
  • No upscaling: Images must be submitted at their native capture resolution. Upscaled images will be disqualified upon RAW verification.
  • Colour space: sRGB preferred. Other colour spaces accepted but converted for scoring.
  • Post-processing: Standard adjustments are permitted — exposure, colour grading, cropping, sharpening, noise reduction, dodging and burning. Heavy compositing, sky replacement, object addition or removal, and any use of generative AI tools are not permitted.
Mobile photographers: Upload your image directly from your phone gallery. Do not run your image through social media or messaging apps before uploading — this strips EXIF data and reduces quality.

04 Prior Publication & Prior Prize Restriction

Prior Publication

Images that have been previously published — on social media, personal websites, stock libraries, editorial outlets, or anywhere else — are fully eligible for entry. There is no restriction on prior publication.

Prior Prize Restriction

Images that have previously won a cash prize in any other photography competition — whether domestic or international — are not eligible for entry in any {{ platform_name }} competition. This restriction applies regardless of the prize amount, the competition's prestige, or how long ago the prize was won.

Declaration required: By entering any competition on {{ platform_name }}, the photographer declares that the submitted image has not previously won a cash prize in any other competition. False declaration will result in immediate disqualification, forfeiture of all prizes, and permanent account suspension.

06 RAW Verification — Finalists & Winners

Verification Requirement

The top 3 finalists in every category across all competitions are required to submit their original RAW or full-resolution source file for authenticity verification before any prize is confirmed or announced.

Submission Process

  • Finalists will be notified by email with instructions to submit their original file.
  • Original files must be uploaded to the platform within the timeframe stated in the notification email.

What Verification Checks

  • EXIF consistency: Camera make, model, date taken, and exposure settings in the original must match the submitted JPEG.
  • Resolution: The original file must demonstrate native capture resolution of at least 2,000px on the shorter side.
  • Authenticity: The submitted JPEG must be a reasonable derivative of the original — standard post-processing is accepted. Evidence of generative AI, heavy compositing, or scene fabrication will result in disqualification.
  • For mobile photographers: The original file from the device gallery is accepted in HEIC, JPEG, or DNG format.

Consequences of Non-Submission or Failure

Failure to submit the original file within the timeframe, or failure of the verification check, results in immediate disqualification. The prize passes to the next placed entry. {{ platform_name }}'s verification decision is final.

Note: Images uploaded to {{ platform_name }} are automatically resized to 960px for AI scoring purposes. The verification process requires your original unresized file — not the platform copy. Always retain your original files.

07 Monthly Programme Rules

  • Contest period: the full calendar month from 00:00 on the 1st to 23:59 on the last day (IST).
  • Results finalised within 10 business days of month close, following completion of Tier 2 peer rating.
  • Winner is the photographer with the highest validated score in each genre and League.
  • In the event of a tied score, the image submitted earlier in the month takes precedence.
  • A photographer may win multiple genre categories in the same month.
  • All top 3 finalists per category are subject to RAW verification before prizes are confirmed (see Section 06).
  • Prizes are disbursed within 30 days of results finalisation, subject to winner verification, age confirmation, and tax compliance.
  • There is no restriction on when the image was originally captured — only the upload must occur within the contest month.

08 Photographer of the Year (POTY) Rules & Prize Structure

  • POTY operates per genre and per League (Camera and Mobile independently) over the platform subscription year. 16 competitions total: 8 genres × 2 Leagues.
  • Minimum eligibility: 24 qualifying images in the specific genre during the subscription year.
  • POTY score: arithmetic mean of the photographer's top 6 DDI scores in that genre across all qualifying images for the year.
  • Grand POTY Camera and Grand POTY Mobile are awarded separately. The Grand POTY winner in each League is selected by jury from the 8 category Gold winners in that League.
  • All top 3 finalists per category are subject to RAW verification before prizes are confirmed (see Section 06).
  • In the event of a tied POTY score, the photographer with the higher individual peak score wins. If still tied, the photographer with the longer subscription tenure wins.

09 Body of Work (BOW) Rules

  • One BOW submission per photographer per year. Open to all active subscribers aged 18 or over.
  • Submission: 6 to 12 images as a unified thematic series. Submissions outside this range are ineligible.
  • All images must have been previously scored by the Apex DDI Engine under the submitting photographer's account.
  • BOW is cross-genre. No League split. Camera and Mobile compete together.
  • Submissions close at 23:59 IST on the last day of Month 11 of the platform year.
  • Judged by Jury Legends only — no peer rating layer. DDI scores are reference data only.
  • All images in the top 3 BOW submissions are subject to RAW verification before prizes are confirmed (see Section 06).

13 Disqualification, Fraud & Integrity

{{ platform_name }} reserves the right to disqualify any entry, void any result, and withhold or recover any prize in the following circumstances:

  • Submission of AI-generated images or images primarily created through generative AI tools
  • Submission of images belonging to another photographer
  • Submission of an image that has previously won a cash prize in another competition
  • Failure to pass RAW verification within the required timeframe
  • Images that do not meet the technical specifications in Section 03
  • Entrant is under 18 years of age
  • Duplicate submission of the same or substantially similar image
  • Manipulation of the peer rating system
  • Multiple account creation for competition advantage
  • Tampering with or stripping EXIF metadata
  • Any conduct that undermines the integrity of the competition

Fraud Penalty

Where fraud is determined — including AI-generated images, stolen images, or falsified RAW files — the following consequences apply immediately and without appeal:

  • All images submitted to the platform are permanently removed
  • The account is permanently deleted and the photographer is banned from registering any future account
  • All prizes won are forfeited — including prizes already disbursed, which must be returned upon demand
  • {{ platform_name }} reserves the right to report the matter to relevant authorities where applicable law is engaged

Random RAW Verification

In addition to mandatory verification of top 3 finalists, {{ platform_name }} conducts random RAW verification spot checks on any image at any time, regardless of score or competition standing. Failure to respond within the stated timeframe, or failure of the verification check, will result in the image being removed and the account reviewed for fraud.

17 Disputes, Arbitration & Jurisdiction

Internal Dispute Process

A participant who believes their image has been incorrectly scored or that a competition result is in error may raise a formal dispute through their dashboard. Each dispute costs one Rating Credit. The same image may not be disputed more than once. Following a Stage 2 jury decision, no further internal appeal is available.

Arbitration

Any dispute arising out of or in connection with these rules that is not resolved through the internal dispute process shall be referred to and finally resolved by arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 (India). The seat and venue of arbitration shall be Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. The language of arbitration shall be English. The arbitrator's award shall be final and binding.

Governing Law & Jurisdiction

These Programme Rules are governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of India. Subject to the arbitration clause above, any dispute that proceeds to court shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts at Bengaluru, Karnataka.
