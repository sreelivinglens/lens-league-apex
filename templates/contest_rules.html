01 Competition Overview

Lens League Apex operates three distinct competition formats across 8 genres (Wildlife, Street, Landscape, People, Wedding, Macro, Drone & Aerial, and Creative) and two tracks (Camera and Mobile):

Competition Frequency Scope Judging
Monthly Contest Every calendar month Per genre · Camera & Mobile separate DDI → Peer → Jury (triggered)
POTY Annual · ₹50L pool Per genre · Camera & Mobile separate DDI → Peer → Jury (triggered)
Open Competition Annual · ₹8L pool All 8 genres · No track split · ₹50/entry DDI → Jury (mandatory for Grand Open)

All competitions are operated on the principle of objective, bias-free evaluation. The Apex DDI Engine provides the first layer of scoring. Human evaluation layers are structured to prevent bias, maintain independence, and ensure legal defensibility of all prize decisions.

02 Eligibility & Entry

General Eligibility

  • Entry is open to all registered Lens League Apex subscribers in good standing.
  • Accounts with active policy violations, pending fraud investigations, or suspended status are ineligible.
  • Employees, directors, and immediate family members of Lens League Apex are ineligible for prize awards, though they may use the platform.
  • Participants must be aged 18 or over, or have verifiable parental/guardian consent where applicable under local law.

Monthly Contest & POTY — Automatic Entry

For Monthly Contests: every image scored by the Apex DDI Engine within the calendar month is automatically entered into the contest for its declared genre and track. No separate entry action is required.

For POTY: all images meeting the minimum qualification threshold (24 images in the genre during the subscription year) are automatically considered. No separate entry action is required.

Open Competition — Paid Entry

The Open Competition requires deliberate, paid entry. Each entry costs ₹50 per image. There is no track split — Camera and Mobile photographers compete in the same pool per genre. A photographer may submit multiple entries across different genres. Entry is available to all active subscribers for the duration the Open Competition is live (opens 3 months before Grand Prix; see Section 6 for full timeline).

Track Declaration (POTY only)

Photographers must declare their track (Camera or Mobile) at subscription. Track determines which POTY competition pool images enter. Images submitted under the incorrect track are ineligible for POTY in that cycle. Track changes take effect from the following subscription month. Track declaration does not apply to the Open Competition, which has no track split.

03 Judging Process & Finality

Three-Tier Structure

All judging proceeds through up to three tiers. For Monthly Contests and POTY, no tier may be bypassed. For the Open Competition, Tier 2 peer rating applies to category-level ranking; Tier 3 jury is mandatory for Grand Open Winner selection.

Tier 1 — Apex DDI Engine

Automated, objective, runs on every image at submission. Produces a numerical DDI score and tier classification. Tier 1 results are used for initial leaderboard ranking and POTY qualification calculations.

Tier 2 — Blind Peer Rating (Monthly, POTY, and Open category ranking)

Each image is assigned to a minimum of five independent peer raters. Assignment is random within stratified tier bands. Raters do not know the photographer's identity, the DDI score, or the identity of other raters for the same image. A minimum of 15 seconds per image is required for a rating to be valid. Outlier ratings deviating more than two standard deviations from peer consensus are excluded and trigger an automatic audit.

Tier 3 — Jury

For Monthly and POTY: jury review is triggered when (a) peer rating diverges from DDI score by more than 1.5 points, or (b) a valid formal dispute is raised by the photographer.

For Open Competition: jury review is mandatory for the Grand Open Winner selection. The jury evaluates all 8 category winners as a shortlist and selects the single Grand Open Winner. Jury composition is never disclosed to photographers or other participants.

Finality of Decisions: Tier 3 jury decisions are final, conclusive, and binding on all parties. No further internal appeal is available following a Tier 3 decision. Lens League Apex's determination of all competition matters — including eligibility, scoring, disqualification, and prize awards — is final.

Jury Legend Qualification

Jury Legends must satisfy one or both of the following criteria: (a) be a platform member who has achieved and maintained Legend tier (DDI score 9.7 or above) for a minimum of 6 consecutive months and has received written approval from Lens League Apex; or (b) be an externally appointed judge selected and contracted directly by Lens League Apex. Jury Legend status may be withdrawn at any time at the discretion of Lens League Apex.

04 Monthly Contest Rules

  • Contest period: the full calendar month from 00:00 on the 1st to 23:59 on the last day (IST).
  • Results are finalised within 10 business days of month close, following completion of Tier 2 peer rating.
  • Winner is the photographer with the highest validated score (post Tier 2, or post Tier 3 where triggered) in each genre and track.
  • In the event of a tied score, the image submitted earlier in the month takes precedence.
  • A photographer may win multiple genre categories in the same month.
  • Leaderboards display tier classification only throughout the contest month. Final results announce winner by username and tier.
  • Prizes, where applicable, are disbursed within 30 days of results finalisation, subject to winner verification and tax compliance.

05 Photographer of the Year (POTY) Rules

  • POTY operates per genre and per track (Camera and Mobile independently) over the platform subscription year. 16 competitions total: 8 genres × 2 tracks.
  • Minimum eligibility: 24 qualifying images evaluated in the specific genre during the subscription year.
  • POTY score: arithmetic mean of the photographer's highest 6 DDI scores in that genre across all qualifying images for the year.
  • A photographer subscribing from Month 1 may contribute up to 48 images; Month 4, up to 32; Month 7, up to 24. The top-6 methodology ensures quality determines outcome, not volume alone.
  • A dynamic percentile threshold is calculated monthly and updated as new qualifying images are submitted. The threshold determines which images enter the active POTY pool. The threshold is recalculated until the final submission deadline of the 12th month.
  • Additional images purchased beyond the monthly subscription allowance are included in POTY evaluation on equal terms at the rates published on the pricing page.
  • Final POTY results are announced within 30 days of the 12th month submission deadline.
  • Grand POTY Camera and Grand POTY Mobile are awarded separately. The Grand POTY winner in each track is selected by jury from the 8 Camera Gold (or 8 Mobile Gold) category winners respectively.
  • In the event of a tied POTY score, the photographer with the higher individual peak score (best single image) in that genre wins. If still tied, the photographer with the longer subscription tenure wins.

06 Open Competition Rules

The Open Competition is an annual single-entry competition open to all active subscribers. It is entirely separate from POTY and Monthly Contests. A photographer may enter both POTY and the Open Competition in the same year.

Entry

  • Entry fee: ₹50 per image. Each entry is for one image in one genre category.
  • A photographer may submit multiple entries across different genres but may submit only one entry per genre.
  • There is no track split. Camera and Mobile photographers compete in the same pool for each genre.
  • Images must have been previously scored by the Apex DDI Engine on the platform under the submitting photographer's account.
  • Entry opens 3 months before the Grand Prix date and closes at 23:59 IST on the date published on the platform. Entries may not be modified after submission.

Categories & Judging

  • Entries are evaluated per genre. The 8 genres are: Wildlife, Street, Landscape, People, Wedding, Macro, Drone & Aerial, and Creative.
  • Category winner selection: DDI score provides initial ranking. Peer rating (Tier 2) validates ranking within each genre. The highest-ranked entry per genre is the category winner.
  • There is one winner per genre. No Silver or Bronze — one winner only.
  • In the event of a tied score, the image with the higher DDI score (pre-peer adjustment) takes precedence. If still tied, the earlier submission wins.

Grand Open Winner

  • The Grand Open Winner is selected by jury from the 8 category winners only. No other entry is eligible for the Grand Open prize.
  • The jury evaluates the 8 shortlisted images as individual works and selects the single best image across all genres.
  • Jury composition is not disclosed. The jury decision is final and binding.
  • The Grand Open Winner receives ₹2,00,000 in addition to their category prize of ₹75,000. Total prize for the Grand Open Winner: ₹2,75,000.

Results

Open Competition results are announced within 30 days of the entry deadline. Prize disbursement follows the conditions in Section 7.

08 Disqualification & Integrity

Lens League Apex reserves the right to disqualify any entry, void any result, and withhold or recover any prize in the following circumstances:

  • Submission of AI-generated images or images primarily created through generative AI tools
  • Submission of images belonging to another photographer
  • Duplicate submission of the same or substantially similar image across multiple entries or months
  • Manipulation of the peer rating system including coordinated voting rings, targeted sabotage, or low-effort rating
  • Multiple account creation for competition advantage
  • Tampering with EXIF metadata or other authenticity markers
  • Any conduct that undermines the integrity of the competition or the platform
  • Violation of these rules or the Terms & Conditions at any point, including after a prize has been awarded

Disqualification decisions are made at the sole discretion of Lens League Apex and are not subject to internal appeal beyond the process described in Section 10.

10 Disputes & Appeals

Raising a Dispute

A participant who believes their image has been incorrectly scored or that a competition result is in error may raise a formal dispute through their dashboard. Each dispute costs one Rating Credit. The same image may not be disputed more than once.

Dispute Process

  • Stage 1: Automated review. The system checks for processing errors, metadata anomalies, or rating outliers. Resolved within 5 business days. If an error is confirmed, it is corrected and the Rating Credit refunded.
  • Stage 2: If Stage 1 finds no error, the dispute is escalated to Tier 3 jury review. The image is evaluated by an independent jury panel whose composition is not disclosed. Jury decision is communicated within 15 business days. The jury decision is final.

No Further Internal Appeal

Following a Stage 2 jury decision, no further internal appeal is available. Participants who remain dissatisfied may seek independent legal remedy as provided under Section 12, subject to the limitation of liability provisions in Section 9.

Dispute Abuse

Filing disputes without reasonable grounds, or filing multiple disputes as a tactic to delay competition results, constitutes a violation of these rules and may result in suspension of dispute privileges or account suspension.

13 Amendments

Lens League Apex reserves the right to amend these Contest Rules at any time. Amendments affecting ongoing competitions will be communicated to all active participants via the platform with a minimum of 14 days notice before taking effect, except where immediate amendment is required to protect the integrity of the competition or comply with applicable law.

Continued participation in any competition following notification of an amendment constitutes acceptance of the amended rules.

In the event of any conflict between these Contest Rules and the Terms & Conditions, these Contest Rules shall prevail in respect of competition matters.
