2. Platform Description

Shutter League is a digital photography skill assessment platform. All services provided are digital in nature and include AI-powered image scoring, feedback, progress tracking, and access to structured photography programmes. No physical goods are sold or shipped.

The Platform uses the Dimensional Depth Index (DDI) engine to evaluate photographs across five dimensions: Depth of Detail, Visual Disruption, Decisive Moment, Wonder Factor, and Affective Quotient. Scores are generated programmatically and are not expressions of personal opinion.
