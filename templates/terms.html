00 — Nature of Services ▾

Shutter League is a software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform providing AI-powered photography skill assessment tools as a digital subscription service. Subscribers pay a recurring fee for access to platform features including automated image scoring, dimensional analysis, personalised improvement recommendations, and community rankings.

The subscription fee represents payment for software tool access only. No physical goods are delivered. No element of chance determines subscription value or scoring outcomes — all assessments are produced by a deterministic AI scoring engine applied uniformly to all submissions.

The term 'League' in the Shutter League name refers to the platform's tiered skill progression system (Rookie through Legend) and does not indicate a gaming, wagering, or chance-based activity of any kind. Photography Programmes available on the platform are skill-based juried evaluations, not lotteries or contests of chance.
