Photography Programmes

Three ways to go further.
One scientific standard.

Your uploaded images build your Annual Excellence Award standing continuously through the calendar year. The Open Submission is an annual deliberate entry. The Body of Work is the platform’s most prestigious annual submission.

Who Can Participate

  • All registered Shutter League members — Camera Users and Mobile Users.
  • Prize eligibility restricted to Indian residents with a valid PAN card and an operational Indian bank account. Participation is open to all; prize payments are governed by Indian law (TDS applicable under Section 194B).
  • All programmes run on the calendar year: 1 January – 31 December. Scores, standings, and qualifying history reset on 1 January each year.
  • Images must be the photographer's original work. AI-generated images, composites, and stolen images are disqualified. RAW file verification applies to all programme recipients.
  • Full submission rules, IP rights, rejection grounds, and recourse process: Programme Rules.

Open Submission Programme
Sep – NovSubmission Window
The Open Submission is a single-entry annual programme with no league split — Camera Users and Mobile Users are evaluated in the same pool. You select one image per genre, pay ₹50 per submission, and participate for genre recognition. The Grand Open Recipient is selected by jury from all 8 genre recipients.

Unlike the Monthly Programme and Annual Excellence Award, the Open has no automatic entry — you choose your best image deliberately and submit it during the September to November window each calendar year.

Body of Work
DecemberSubmission Window
The Body of Work is the platform's most prestigious programme — and the hardest to enter. You submit 6 to 12 images as a single unified thematic series in one genre or subject of your choice. Not a collection. A series with a narrative arc, a visual language, a point of view.

Your chosen genre or subject does not need to match the platform's standard categories — submissions in Environment, Conservation, Humanity, Water, Street, or any cohesive photographic subject are welcome. Evaluated exclusively by Jury Legends. No peer rating.
