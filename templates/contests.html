Monthly Contest

Every image you upload is automatically entered into the contest for its genre and track — no separate action required. Camera and Mobile tracks are judged independently. Annual · Paid Entry
Open Competition
₹8LTotal Prize Pool
The Open Competition is a single-entry annual competition with no track split — Camera and Mobile photographers compete in the same pool. You select one image per genre, pay ₹50 per entry, and compete for the category prize. The Grand Open Winner is selected by jury from all 8 category winners.

Unlike the Monthly Contest and POTY, the Open has no automatic entry — you choose your best image deliberately and enter it.
₹75,000 Category Winner (×8 genres)
₹2,00,000 Grand Open Winner
₹50 per entry
Entries open 3 months before December each year. You will be notified when the Open Competition window opens.
