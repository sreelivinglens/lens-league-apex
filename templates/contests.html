Photographer of the Year

Annual · Open Submission
Open Photography Programme
₹ 8,00,000Annual Honours
The Open Programme is a single-entry annual submission with no League split — Camera and Mobile photographers are evaluated in the same pool. You select one image per genre, pay ₹50 per submission, and participate for genre recognition. The Grand Open recipient is selected by jury from all 8 genre recipients.

Unlike the Monthly Programme and POTY, the Open has no automatic entry — you choose your best image deliberately and submit it.
₹ 75,000 Genre Recipient (×8 genres)
₹ 2,00,000 Grand Open Recipient
₹ 50 per submission
Submissions open 3 months before December each year. You will be notified when the Open Programme window opens.
